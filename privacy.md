# Privacy Policy — Live Metal Price Update

**Effective date:** March 2025

---

## Who we are

**Live Metal Price Update** is a Shopify app that lets merchants sync their product prices to live silver and gold rates and update variant prices in EUR. The app is operated by **Flame Forge** (flameforge.work).

---

## Data we collect and use

We collect and use only what is needed to run the app.

- **Shop and account.** We receive your shop domain and Shopify session/access so the app can run in your admin and call Shopify on your behalf.

- **App settings (stored by us).** When you configure the app, we store:
  - Your Metal Price API key (so we can fetch silver/gold rates)
  - Default markup percentage and price-update schedule (e.g. manual, daily, or hourly)
  - A secure token used for scheduled “cron” price updates

- **Shopify data we read and write.** We read and write product and variant data (e.g. IDs, prices) and product/shop metafields (e.g. weight, margin, silver/gold rates) only to calculate and update prices. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

---

## Why we use this data

We use the data above to:

- Fetch current metal rates from the Metal Price API  
- Compute and apply prices to your products  
- Run scheduled price updates when you have enabled them  
- Remember your app configuration (API key, markup, schedule)  
- Operate the app securely (e.g. session and cron token)

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

---

## Third parties

- **Shopify.** Your store runs on Shopify. They host your store and provide the APIs we use. Shopify’s own privacy policy applies to how they handle store and merchant data.

- **Metal Price API (metalpriceapi.com).** We send requests to this service to get current silver and gold rates. We do not send any merchant or customer personal data to Metal Price API—only what is required to retrieve rates.

- **Hosting and database.** Session data and app settings are stored on our (or our host’s) servers. We use industry-standard security practices and use this data only to operate the app.

---

## Data retention

We keep your shop identifier and app settings while the app is installed. After you uninstall the app, we delete or anonymize this data within 90 days, except where we are required to keep it for legal or accounting reasons.

---

## Your rights

You can:

- **Uninstall the app** at any time. This stops any new use of your data by the app.  
- **Request access** to the data we hold about your shop.  
- **Request deletion** of that data, subject to legal or accounting requirements.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in the **Contact** section below.

---

## Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the current version at **https://flameforge.work/p/livemetalupdate/privacy** and update the effective date when we make changes. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
